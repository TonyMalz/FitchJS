Fitch Rules

Conjunction Introduction (∧ Intro)

Description:

If you have two ore more formulae i.e. Tuesday and Raining in two separate lines you can combine those predicates in one line by connecting them with a logical AND (∧) like so: Tuesday ∧ Raining.
Also the order of appearence does not matter, as Raining ∧ Tuesday is also a valid conclusion.
In general the symbol ϕ stands for any well formed formula within the current proof.
